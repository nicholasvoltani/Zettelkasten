Given a group $G$ and an element $g \in G$, the **left** coset $gG$ is defined as
$$gG = \{gk \mid \forall k \in G\}$$
and the **right** coset $gG$ is defined as 
$$Gg = \{kg \mid \forall k \in G\}$$
